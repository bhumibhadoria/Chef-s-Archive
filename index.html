Butter Chicken

Marinate the chicken with yogurt, spices, and lemon juice for at least 30 minutes. Cook onions, tomatoes, and spices until soft and aromatic. Add marinated chicken and cook until tender. Add cream and butter, and simmer until the sauce thickens.

Palak Paneer

Blanch spinach and blend to a puree. Fry onions, garlic, and tomatoes with spices. Add spinach puree and paneer cubes, and simmer until well combined. Finish with cream and garam masala.

Biryani

Marinate meat with yogurt, spices, and lemon juice. Cook basmati rice with whole spices. Layer marinated meat and partially cooked rice, and cook on low heat until fully cooked and aromatic.

Chole (Chickpea Curry)

Soak and boil chickpeas until tender. Cook onions, tomatoes, and spices. Add chickpeas and water, and simmer until the gravy thickens. Garnish with coriander and serve hot.

Dosa

Prepare a batter with fermented rice and urad dal. Pour a thin layer on a hot griddle to form a crispy crepe. Serve with sambar and coconut chutney.
