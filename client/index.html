Welcome to Chef's Archives! Explore our collection of delicious recipes.

Butter Chicken

Marinate the chicken with yogurt, spices, and lemon juice for at least 30 minutes. Cook onions, tomatoes, and spices until soft and aromatic. Add marinated chicken and cook until tender.

Palak Paneer

Blanch spinach and blend to a puree. Fry onions, garlic, and tomatoes with spices. Add spinach puree and paneer cubes, and simmer until well combined.

Biryani

Marinate meat with yogurt, spices, and lemon juice. Cook basmati rice with whole spices. Layer marinated meat and partially cooked rice, and cook on low heat until fully cooked and aromatic.

Chole (Chickpea Curry)

Soak and boil chickpeas until tender. Cook onions, tomatoes, and spices. Add chickpeas and water, and simmer until the gravy thickens. Garnish with coriander and serve hot.

Aloo Tikki Chaat

Prepare mashed potato patties, fry them until golden, and serve with yogurt, chutneys, and chole.

Dosa

Prepare a batter with fermented rice and urad dal. Pour a thin layer on a hot griddle to form a crispy crepe. Serve with sambar and coconut chutney.

Kachori

Prepare dough and filling with spiced lentils. Shape into small rounds and deep fry until crispy and golden.

Paneer Tikka

Marinate paneer cubes in yogurt and spices. Grill or bake until charred and serve with mint chutney.

Dal Makhani

Cook black lentils and kidney beans with spices and cream. Simmer until thickened and serve with naan or rice.
